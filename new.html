Welcome to Our College

The Higher Institute of Engineering and Technology

located in Mansoura was established in 2005 in accordance with Ministerial Resolution No.(1307) of 2005, amended by No. (504) of 2006, with the aim of preparing distinguished and specialized graduates to work in the fields of civil engineering, communications engineering, computers, and architectural engineering, and to be qualified to work and deal with environmental variables and able to deal with the open labor market in light of globalization and what it requires in terms of creative and innovative capabilities.
